# Panang Curry Paste

# Ingredients
**Dried:**
- 2 t coriander
- .5 t cumin
- 3 pieces mace
- 3 dried green chillies (30 g)
- 2 green cardamom
- .5 t black peppercorns
- .5 t salt
- 12 dried big red chillies (sub guajillo or pasilla)

**Fresh:**
- 10 g galangal
- 10 g lemongrass
- 5 g kaffir lime peel
- 10 g coriander root
- 15 g shallots
- 10 g garlic
- 5 g shrimp paste
